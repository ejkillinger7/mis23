Hobbies, Family, Favorite Sports Teams

Posted by Ethyn Killinger on May 2, 2023

Hobbies

Fishing: Fishing is one of my favorite hobbies when I need to relax or reset. I enjoy just being out on the water, regardless if I am actually catching fish...that is just a bonus. I enjoy fishing for Largemouth and Smallmouth Bass all over eastern Washignton. A few of my favorite lakes are Lake Roosevelt, Lake Spokane, and Horseshoe lake.

Golfing: Golfing is currently my hobby of choice. I grew up playing baseball which made the transition to hitting a golf ball difficult, however I have immproved a ton over the years. Golfing seems to be every ex-baseball players hobby of choice since you get to hit the ball far. A couple of my favorite courses in the Spokane area are Wandermere, Latah creek, and Qualchan.

Space: I have always been deeply fascinated by space and the mysteries that are still out there. I think there is so much to learn and we barely know a fraction of it. I also think space holds answers to some of the driving questions of humanity such as what our purpose is. Until just recently I planned on puruing a career in Astronomy, but changed my mind and looked forward to a carrer in business finance.

Family

Family is the most important thing in my life and I was lucky enough to have been raised with and by the most amazing people I have ever known. I have a little brother who has always been by my side and is going to college next year and playing college baseball as a pitcher. My family is very close and we are all lucky to be so important in eachothers lives.
